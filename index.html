Paw-some Pet Sitting

Paw-some provides services for cats and dogs of all breeds and ages.

Paw-some offers pet sitting services when you plan to go on vacation, travel for holidays, need to work late, have an illness or injury, or have new puppies or kittens that required extra care. Paw-some pet sitting provides exercise, attention, fresh food, clean water for your pets when you can not, and even cleans up wastes or takes your pet for a potty break.
